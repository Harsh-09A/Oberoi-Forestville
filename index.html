OBEROI THANE - FORESTVILLE

Forestville Kolshet by Oberoi Realty (Oberoi thane launch) is located in the most Seamless environment Kolshet Road, Thane West. Forestville offers you a sensorial treat by recreating opportunities to experience life close to water. Forestville Comes with Luxurious 3 & 4 BHK Residences with the availability of Jodi Apartments—Immerse in a world of elegance and sophistication.
Homes at Forestville are rooted in modern design and human centricity that blends form and function seamlessly. Forestville Kolshet is a 60-storey building that finds a balance between Nature and Luxury. Built on the strong foundation of Biophilia, Forestville Kolshet will inspire quality life in harmony with the vast expanse of nature. It has 5 towers where nature and architecture come together beautifully to elevate every day.
